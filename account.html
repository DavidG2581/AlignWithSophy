Account Management

Update your membership, payment details, and personal information in one place.

Manage Profile

Membership Status

Use the Member Portal to toggle between free and paid tiers, pause billing, or cancel your plan entirely. The portal connects directly to your Amazon Cognito User Pool and payment provider so your access level updates immediately after checkout.

  • Open the Members page to review premium benefits.
  • After confirming payment, refresh your profile under Member Login to unlock premium-only content.
  • Cancelling your subscription automatically downgrades your Cognito account to the free tier while preserving your practice history.

Profile Information

Keep your contact details current so we can send practice reminders and billing notices. Any updates you make here sync with Amazon Cognito attributes.

Update Contact Details

Visit the Member Login section to update your name, email, and date of birth. The portal recalculates your age automatically whenever you adjust your birthday.

Change Password

Use the password reset option provided by Amazon Cognito to set a new password. Be sure to follow the password rules enforced during sign-up (minimum 8 characters, 2 uppercase letters, 2 numbers, and 1 special character).

Update Payment Method

Our third-party billing tool manages your payment information securely. Launch the billing portal to add a new card, update an expiration date, or download receipts.

  • Once payment changes are confirmed, Cognito attribute triggers can flag your account as paid without any manual intervention.
  • For failed payments, the system automatically notifies you and temporarily restricts premium access until the issue is resolved.

Delete Account

Need to close your account? Deleting your profile permanently removes your Cognito user record and any saved preferences. Premium access will terminate immediately.

  • Contact support to verify your identity before an account deletion request is processed.
  • Automate deletions by wiring the dropdown delete option to a protected API that calls Cognito AdminDeleteUser.
